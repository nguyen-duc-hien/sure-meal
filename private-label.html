A Commitment to Quality

SureMeal™ products are proudly made in the USA, ensuring they meet the highest of quality standards, including:

cGMP

This refers to the “Current Good Manufacturing Practice” regulations enforced by the FDA. CGMPs provide for systems that assure proper design, monitoring, and control of manufacturing processes and facilities

NSF

“National Sanitation Foundation”, is a non-profit organization founded in 1944, whose goal was to create standards for food safety and sanitation to promote public health. When you purchase NSF certified foodservice products, it means: The manufacturer of foodservice products uses only FDA approved raw materials.

FDA Registered Facilities

We are subjected to yearly random inspections to make sure our building is up to "food manufacturing" standards.
